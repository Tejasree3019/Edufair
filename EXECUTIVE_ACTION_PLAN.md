## 🎯 KEY SUCCESS FACTORS

### 1. Speed to Market
- Launch features weekly (not monthly)
- Iterate based on user feedback
- A/B test everything

### 2. Community Building
- First 1K users = superusers (hand-hold them)
- Referral rewards = viral growth
- Success stories = social proof

### 3. Quality Assurance
- 99.9% uptime = mandatory (user trust critical)
- Mobile-first (80% users on mobile)
- Accessibility = WCAG AA compliant

### 4. Data Privacy
- GDPR/CCPA compliant from day 1
- No data selling
- Transparent privacy policy
- Encryption at rest + transit

### 5. Strategic Partnerships
- Universities (for listings)
- Employers (for sponsored scholarships)
- Financial services (for affiliate revenue)
- Tech platforms (for OAuth, payments)

## ⚠️ RISKS & MITIGATION

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|-----------|
| Copycats copy features fast | High | Medium | Focus on unique IP (algorithms, community), patent key innovations |
| Scholarship data becomes stale | Medium | High | Auto-crawl websites, community submissions, university API partnerships |
| User growth slower than expected | Medium | High | Aggressive marketing, influencer partnerships, PR campaigns |
| Regulatory issues in SE Asia | Medium | High | Hire local legal counsel, partner with governments |
| Team burnout at scale | High | Medium | Hire strong team, delegate early, build systems not heroes |
| Payment/fraud issues | Low | High | Use proven payment processors (Stripe), built-in fraud detection |
