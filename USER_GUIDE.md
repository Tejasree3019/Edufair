## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

**Step 1: Sign Up**
1. Visit edufair.com
2. Click "Register" button
3. Enter email address
4. Create secure password (min. 8 characters)
5. Click "Sign Up"

**Step 2: Email Verification**
1. Check your email inbox
2. Click verification link
3. Account activated
4. Ready to login

### First Login

**Step 1: Log In**
1. Go to edufair.com/login
2. Enter email & password
3. Click "Login"
4. You're on the dashboard

**Step 2: Complete Profile**
1. Click "Complete Profile"
2. Fill in required information:
   - Full name
   - Date of birth
   - Phone number
   - Current school/college
   - Location (city/state)
3. Click "Next"

**Step 3: Academic Information**
1. Enter current grade/year
2. GPA or percentage score
3. Board/University name
4. Field of study preference
5. Click "Save"

**Step 4: Upload Documents**
1. Upload identity proof (required)
   - Aadhaar, PAN, or Driver's License
2. Upload address proof (required)
   - Electricity bill or Rent agreement
3. Upload academic transcript (optional)
4. Save changes

**Congratulations!** Your profile is ready. You can now search scholarships.

**18-Field Comprehensive Form:**

**Section 1: Personal Information**

**Section 2: Contact Information**
- Email address*
- Phone number*
- City/State*

**Section 3: Academic Background**

*Required fields

### Step-by-Step Application

**Step 1: Review Scholarship**
1. Read all requirements
2. Check if you're eligible
3. Click "Apply Now"

**Step 2: Enter Personal Details**
1. Form appears with your profile info pre-filled
2. Update any incorrect information
3. Click "Next"

**Step 3: Upload Documents**
1. Upload identity proof (required)

3. Upload transcript (optional)

**Step 4: Answer Questions**

3. Click "Next"

1. List special achievements
2. Add extracurricular activities
3. Add additional information
4. Click "Next"

**Step 6: Review & Submit**
1. Review all entered information
2. Check all documents uploaded
3. Accept terms & conditions
4. Click "Submit Application"
5. **Success!** See confirmation

### Important Tips

✅ **DO:**
- Complete all required fields
- Upload clear, readable documents
- Be honest in essay responses
- Proofread before submitting
- Keep copies of documents
- Apply early before deadline
- Apply to multiple scholarships

❌ **DON'T:**
- Leave required fields blank

- Apply for scholarships you don't qualify for

### After Submission

## Tracking Applications

Your dashboard shows:
- **Total Applications**: All applications submitted
- **Submitted**: Currently in system
- **Under Review**: Being evaluated
- **Accepted**: Won scholarships ✅
- **Rejected**: Not accepted ❌

### Application List

Each application card shows:
- Scholarship name

- Last update
- Match percentage
- Application ID

**Status Explanations:**
- 🔵 **Submitted**: Received, waiting for review
- 🟡 **Under Review**: Being evaluated by scholarship committee
- 🟢 **Accepted**: Congratulations! You won!
- 🔴 **Rejected**: Application was not selected

## Understanding Recommendations

### Personalized Recommendations

EduFair analyzes your profile and suggests scholarships:

1. Click "Recommended for You"
2. See scholarships ranked by match percentage
3. Match percentage shows fit (0-100%)

### How Recommendations Work

**Our Algorithm Considers:**
- Your academic performance (GPA)
- Your field of study
- Your location (country, state)
- Your age & background
- Your special achievements
- Available scholarships' requirements

**Match Score Calculation:**
- Academic fit: 30%
- Field match: 20%
- Location: 20%
- Age/background: 20%
- Special factors: 10%

**Example:**
```
Academic: 28/30 (strong GPA)
Field: 20/20 (perfect match)
Location: 18/20 (India)
Age: 20/20 (meets age)
Special: 8/10 (some achievements)
─────────────────────────
Total: 94/100 (94% match!)
```

### Why Recommendations Change

Recommendations update when:
- You update your profile
- New scholarships are added
- You apply for scholarships
- Deadlines change

### Using Recommendations

1. **Sort by Match**: Apply to highest matches first
2. **Read "Why" Reason**: Understand the match
3. **Check Deadline**: See time remaining
4. **Apply Early**: Don't wait for deadline

### Notification Channels

You receive notifications via:

**1. Email**
- Receives detailed messages
- Can click link to view application

**2. SMS (Optional)**
- Quick text message alerts
- For urgent deadlines
- Enable in settings

**3. In-App**
- Notifications appear on dashboard
- See all notifications in notification center
- Can mark as read/archive

### Managing Notifications

**Access Notification Center:**
1. Click notification icon (🔔) in top-right
2. See all recent notifications
3. Unread notifications appear first

**Mark as Read:**
1. Click notification
2. It's marked as read
3. Or click "Mark All as Read"

**Delete Notification:**
1. Click notification
2. Click trash icon
3. Notification removed

**Notification Settings:**
1. Click your profile → Settings
2. Go to "Notifications"
3. Choose notification preferences:
   - Email: On/Off
   - SMS: On/Off
   - In-app: On/Off
4. Choose which types to receive
5. Save settings

## FAQ & Troubleshooting

### General Questions

**Q: How many scholarships can I apply for?**
A: No limit! Apply to as many as you qualify for.

**Q: Can I apply after the deadline?**
A: No, applications close at deadline. Set reminders!

**Q: Are all scholarships free to apply?**
A: Yes! All scholarships on EduFair are completely free.

**Q: Do I need to pay any fees?**
A: Never. EduFair and all listed scholarships are free.

**Q: How long does it take to get a decision?**
A: Usually 10-30 days after submission.

### Account Questions

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on login page, check email for reset link.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**
A: Yes, go to Settings → Account and update your email.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account (cannot be undone).

**Q: Can multiple people use one account?**
A: No, each person needs their own account.

### Application Questions

**Q: Can I edit my application after submission?**
A: No, but you can contact support for urgent changes.

**Q: What if I upload wrong documents?**
A: Contact the scholarship provider directly.

**Q: Can I withdraw my application?**
A: Yes, click "Withdraw" on application details page.

**Q: What if my application is rejected?**
A: Review feedback, apply to other scholarships. You can apply again next year.

### Technical Issues

**Q: I can't log in to my account**
A: 
1. Try "Forgot Password" to reset
2. Clear browser cache
3. Try different browser
4. Contact support if still issues

**Q: Scholarships aren't loading**
A:
1. Refresh the page (Ctrl+R)
2. Clear browser cache
3. Try different browser
4. Check internet connection

**Q: My application won't submit**
A:
1. Check all required fields filled
2. Reduce document file size if large
3. Try different browser
4. Contact support

**Q: I'm not receiving emails**
A:
1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Verify email address in profile
3. Enable email notifications in settings
4. Contact support

### Scholarship Questions

**Q: What's a match percentage?**
A: Shows how well you fit the scholarship (0-100%). Higher = better fit.

**Q: Are the scholarships real?**
A: Yes! All scholarships verified from government, universities, and foundations.

**Q: Can international students apply?**
A: Some scholarships accept international students. Check eligibility.

**Q: What if I don't meet all requirements?**
A: Apply anyway! Many scholarships are flexible on some requirements.
